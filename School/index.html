Our Vision

To demonstrate our motto "Train and Pray" by combining sound, academic training and Godly knowledge.

To run a school where God reigns and rules supreme and pleasing Him counts.

Our Mission

To raise children who excel in academics and are deliberately taught to love God.

To maintain good academic standards at all times by having quality staff always, combined with good supervision of children's performances.

To inculcate in children a love for education and afear of God that will build them into mature, successful adults and dependable citizens of our nation.

Conclusion is to produce an end-time army of God whose members are academically, spiritaually, physically and mentally sound. These are exemplary future leaders who will be successful in every endeavour and positively influence our ever-changing world.
